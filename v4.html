Iyke Nova manufactures across six product categories: Cookies, Crackers (our largest range with 19 products), Nuts, PaPa Hut natural nutrient biscuits, Short Cake, and Wafers — 36 products in total, all made in fully automated, NAFDAC-certified facilities.

Yes — all Iyke Nova products are NAFDAC certified and approved by relevant Nigerian food authority bodies. We also run ongoing Quality Improvement Programs (QIPs) to continuously uphold and exceed national food safety standards.
